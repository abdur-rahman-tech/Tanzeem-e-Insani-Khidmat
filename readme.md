## 🎯 Problem Statement & Solution

### 🚨 The Problem
Rural mountain communities in Shishi Valley face severe infrastructural and administrative challenges:
* **Water Supply Shortages:** Unmanaged gravity-fed water pipelines across high-altitude villages.
* **Housing Distress:** Destitute families, orphans, and widows living in damaged homes with no financial means for construction labor.
* **Aid Duplication:** Lack of centralized record-keeping causing unequal *Rashan* (food package) distribution.
* **Volunteer Deployment:** Inefficient manual tracking when organizing local skilled labor (masons, electricians, drivers).

### 💡 The Solution
**Tanzeem-e-Insani Khidmat (TIK) Smart Welfare Platform** digitizes rural non-profit operations. The app allows administrators to manage beneficiary records, organize zero-cost volunteer house building, track community water lines, register local volunteers, and leverage Google Gemini AI to draft bilingual donor appeals and project reports.
